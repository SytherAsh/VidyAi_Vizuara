# Maharana Pratap: Comic Storyline

## Overview
This comic storyline is a biographical account of the life of Maharana Pratap, the king of the Kingdom of Mewar in north-western India. The story follows Pratap's early life, his accession to the throne, and his resistance against the expansionist policy of the Mughal Emperor Akbar, culminating in the Battle of Haldighati. The comic will explore Pratap's bravery, loyalty, and dedication to his kingdom, as well as his strategic use of guerrilla warfare to defeat the Mughals.

## Main Characters

* Maharana Pratap: The protagonist of the story, a brave and determined king who resists the Mughal Empire's expansion.
* Emperor Akbar: The Mughal Emperor who seeks to conquer Mewar and incorporate it into his empire.
* Rani Dheer Bai Bhatiyani: Pratap's mother, who plays a crucial role in his early life and accession to the throne.
* Jagmal Singh: Pratap's younger brother, who is favored by their mother to succeed their father.
* Amar Singh I: Pratap's eldest son, who succeeds him as king.
* Raja Man Singh: A Mughal general who serves under Emperor Akbar and is tasked with defeating Pratap.
