# Scene 3 Narration - Maharana Pratap

**Style:** dramatic
**Tone:** engaging

## Narration Text
(In a deep, dramatic voice)

"In the palace of Mewar, a shadow falls across the throne, as Maharana Udai Singh II's frailty becomes apparent. His courtiers and advisors gather around, their whispers a testament to the treacherous waters of succession. Rani Dheer Bai Bhatiyani, the Maharana's mother, casts a calculating gaze, her heart set on Jagmal Singh, her favored son. But amidst the intrigue, a young Pratap stands tall, his determination and bravery a beacon of hope in a sea of uncertainty. As the fate of Mewar hangs in the balance, the question on everyone's lips is: will the brave young warrior be crowned, or will the crown be claimed by the cunning Jagmal?"
