# Scene 10 Narration - Sambhaji

**Style:** dramatic

## Narration Text
As the sun sets over the ravaged landscape, Rajaram stands resolute, gazing out upon the remnants of a once-mighty fort. The warm glow of the setting sun casts a sense of hope and renewal over the scene, a stark contrast to the turmoil that has ravaged the Maratha Empire. Sambhaji's legacy lives on through his half-brother, who now bears the weight of leading their people forward. The air is thick with the sense of loss and sacrifice, yet an undercurrent of determination pulses through the atmosphere. Rajaram's resolve is palpable, a beacon of hope in a time of darkness. As the empire teeters on the brink of collapse, he stands firm, a testament to the enduring spirit of the Marathas. The future is uncertain, but one thing is clear: Sambhaji's story will not be forgotten.
