# Scene 3 Narration - Sambhaji

**Style:** dramatic
**Tone:** engaging

## Narration Text
As night descends upon Panhala Fort, Sambhaji seizes his moment, scaling down the imposing walls with calculated urgency. The darkness is punctuated only by the faint glow of torches, casting long, ominous shadows on the ground below. Guards patrol the ramparts, their vigilant eyes straining to pierce the veil of night, as Sambhaji's fingers dig into the stone, his feet finding holds in the rugged facade. With each precarious step, he defies the fate that has held him captive, driven by an unyielding determination to claim his rightful place upon the throne. The air is thick with tension, the silence broken only by the soft scrape of his movements, as Sambhaji wills himself to freedom, and the destiny that awaits him. Tonight, he escapes not just his prison, but the shackles of his past, forging a path towards a future fraught with challenge and glory.
